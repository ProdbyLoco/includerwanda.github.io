Rwandan Inclusive Empowerment for Exceptional Children Club

  • Mission

Welcome to INCLUDE, where we are dedicated to creating an inclusive and supportive learning environment for children with physical disabilities in Rwandan schools. We believe in unlocking the true potential of every exceptional child and embracing the diversity that makes each individual unique. Our mission is to break down the barriers of stigma and stereotypes that hinder the success of exceptional children, ensuring they have equal access to education and opportunities. At INCLUDE, we are committed to bringing smiles to millions of exceptional children, fostering a brighter future for each and every one of them.
Our mission at INCLUDE, is to empower children with physical disabilities and promote inclusivity in Rwandan schools. We strive to create a learning environment that is free from stigma and stereotypes, where exceptional children can thrive academically and emotionally. Through our comprehensive platform and dedicated school club, we offer a multi-faceted approach to address the challenges faced by these children, providing them with emotional support, policy awareness, and educational opportunities. We aim to build a more inclusive society where every exceptional child is embraced for their unique abilities and contributions.
At INCLUDE, our vision is to create a world where every exceptional child is given the opportunity to shine and reach their full potential. We envision a future where all children, regardless of physical disabilities, are welcomed and included in the learning environment with open arms. Through our efforts, we aspire to break down the barriers that limit their access to education and hinder their overall well-being. We dream of a society that celebrates diversity and embraces every exceptional child, nurturing their growth, and paving the way for a more inclusive and compassionate world.

What We Do?

Emotional Support

Through our secure counseling chat space, children can seek emotional support and professional counseling from our trained experts.

Policy Awareness

We provide educational materials on disability rights and policies in Rwanda, ensuring that these children are aware of their legal protections.

Peer Connection

Our platform facilitates online communication among children with similar disabilities, creating a virtual community where they can share experiences, offer support, and find understanding in one another.

Advocacy Workshops

Within schools, we organize workshops and seminars to raise awareness about the challenges faced by children with physical disabilities.

Community Engagement

We actively engage in community events, such as disability awareness campaigns and social initiatives, to promote understanding and empathy for children with disabilities beyond the school premises.

Fundraising Activities

Our club organizes fundraising events like bake sales, talent shows, and sports tournaments to raise funds.

What We Do?

Emotional Support

Through our secure counseling chat space, children can seek emotional support and professional counseling from our trained experts.

Policy Awareness

We provide educational materials on disability rights and policies in Rwanda, ensuring that these children are aware of their legal protections.

Peer Connection

Our platform facilitates online communication among children with similar disabilities, creating a virtual community where they can share experiences, offer support, and find understanding in one another.

Advocacy Workshops

Within schools, we organize workshops and seminars to raise awareness about the challenges faced by children with physical disabilities.

Community Engagement

We actively engage in community events, such as disability awareness campaigns and social initiatives, to promote understanding and empathy for children with disabilities beyond the school premises.

Fundraising Activities

Our club organizes fundraising events like bake sales, talent shows, and sports tournaments to raise funds.

Problem Statement

More than 60% of children with physical disabilities in Rwandan schools lack access to an inclusive environment, hindering their academic progress and overall well-being. This exclusion can lead to feelings of isolation, vulnerability, and limited opportunities for these exceptional children.
